HOW DOES SWAP-FREE TRADING WORK?

In conventional forex trading, overnight positions accrue interest based on the interest rate differentials between the currencies involved. However, with our Swap-Free trading accounts, you can hold positions for an extended period without being subject to interest charges or payments. Instead, we provide an alternative mechanism that ensures your trading activities remain compliant with Islamic principles.

To facilitate swap-free trading, we use an account structure that replaces the interest component with administrative fees or adjusts the spreads accordingly. This way, you can participate in the forex market in adherence to Islamic religious principles.

The Benefits of Swap-Free Trading
  • Shariah Compliance: Our swap-free trading accounts are designed to cater to the specific needs of traders who seek to conduct their trading activities in compliance with Islamic principles. By removing interest charges, we provide an ethical and inclusive trading environment.
  • No Overnight Rollover: With Swap-Free trading, you can hold positions overnight or for an extended period without incurring any interest charges. This allows you to maintain your trades in line with your trading strategy and objectives.
  • Flexibility and Convenience: Swap-Free accounts offer the same features and benefits as our other account types, ensuring that you have access to a wide range of trading instruments, advanced trading platforms, real-time market data, and dedicated customer support.
  • Transparency: We are committed to transparency in our Swap-Free trading offering. Our administrative fees or adjusted spreads are clearly communicated, ensuring you have a clear understanding of the costs associated with maintaining positions beyond the daily trading session.

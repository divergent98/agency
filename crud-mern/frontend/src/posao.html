We are happy to announce that we are now part of the Women’s Empowerment Principles Community!

As an international company that is constantly growing, we proudly highlight the fact that more than 60% of employees are women. That is why we strive to empower more women to be very successful in business, and we recognize the great work WEPs Community is doing.

In this regard, Finveo’s joining to the WEPs Community is very important step to making the business world better, and encouraging women to set high career goals.

Finveo has adopted the United Nations Women’s Empowerment Principles (WEPs) as part of its commitment to promoting gender equality and women’s empowerment in the workplace, marketplace and community.
